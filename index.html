About SDG 3

Why health & well-being matter

SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being) is a universal call to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for people of all ages. From reducing maternal and child mortality to fighting communicable diseases and mental health, this goal tackles the root causes of health crises.

Our platform connects communities with emergency support, general wellness services, and specialized care — accelerating progress toward universal health coverage (UHC).

End epidemics (HIV, TB, malaria)
Reduce non-communicable diseases
